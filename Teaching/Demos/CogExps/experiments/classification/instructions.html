Categorization Task

[NOTE: This lab is just a protoype; needs to be fixed up before running live]

This is a classic demonstration of category learning. On each trial, you will be shown an object. Your job is to figure out what category it belongs to. You will be told whether your guess was correct or incorrect after.

At first it will be tricky; but overtime you'll start to learn which objects belong to which categories.
